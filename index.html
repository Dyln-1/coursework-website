Understanding CARP Design Principles

CARP stands for Contrast, Alignment, Repetition, and Proximity — four simple but powerful ideas used in design. These principles help organize content in a way that's both visually appealing and easy to read. Whether you're building a website, designing a poster, or working on a presentation, keeping CARP in mind can make a big difference.

Contrast draws attention to important elements by making them stand out. Alignment keeps things neat and creates a clean, professional look. Repetition adds consistency across your design, while proximity groups related items together so they’re easier to understand. Using these techniques helps your work look more polished and communicates your message more clearly.
